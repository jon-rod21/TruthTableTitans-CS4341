Parts List:

Dec4x16:
    decodes a 4 bit opcode to a 16 bit onehot number for selection

Mux16x8: 
    selects 1 of 16 input signals and forwards it to one of 8 output lines 

FourBitMul   
    behaviouraly multiplies 2 4 bit numbers

FourBitAddSub
    uses the full adder with 2 4 bit numbers and a carry signal to determine addition or subtraction within the full adder

FourBitDiv   
    Uses behavioral division to divide 2 inputs A and B while erroring if division is by 0

FourBitMod   
    Uses behavioral modulus to mod 2 inputs A and B, erroring if modding by 0

FourBitBuffer
    Stores inputA and passes it to another part of the circuit through inputC

FourBitNOT   
    Negates the inputA and returns it into outputC.

FourBitAND   
    Ands inputA and inputB, returning the result in outputC

FourBitNAND  
    Nands inputA and inputB, returing the result in outputC

FourBitOR    
    Or's inputA and inputB, returning the result in outputC

FourBitNOR   
    Nor's inputA and inputB, returning the result in outputC

FourBitXOR   
    XOr's inputA and inputB, returning the result in outputC

FourBitXNOR  
    Xnor's inputA and inputB, returning the result in outputC



Input List:

clk: 
    Signal that synchronizes breadboard operations with a rising and falling edge

rst:
    Signal that resets the state of the breadboard

A:
    K-bit sized operand that is the second operand for operations.

B:
    K-bit sized operand that is the first operand for operations. Is determined mainly by the accumulator.


opcode:
    4 Bit code that determines what logical or arithmetic operation will be performed.


Output List:

C:
    2 * K sized bit that holds the result of the performed operation.

error:
   2 bit error flag that that indicated whether an overflow or divide by zero error has occured.



Interface List:

channels:
    Connects the outputs of all arithmetic/logic units to the multiplexor input. It carries 16 possible operations to the MUX.

select:
    Connects the decoder output to the multiplexor select input channels. Carries the decoded opsignal to determine which channel is selected.

b:
    Connects multiplexor output to accumulator input and output C. Carries selected operation result.

outputAND:
    Connects FourBitAND to output channel 7. Carries the bitwise AND of regB and regA.

outputXOR:
    Connects FourBitXOR to output channel 10. Carries the bitwise XOR of regB and regA.

outputOR:
    Connects FourBitOR to output channel 8. Carries the bitwise OR of regB and regA.

outputNOT:
    Connects FourBitNOT to output channel 9. Carries the bitwise NOT of regB and regA.

outputNAND:
    Connects FourBitNAND to output channel 11. Carries the bitwise NAND of regB and regA.

outputXNOR:
    Connects FourBitXNOR to output channel 13. Carries the bitwise XNOR of regB and regA.

outputNOR:
    Connects FourBitNOR to output channel 12. Carries the bitwise NOR of regB and regA.

outputBUFFER:
    Connects FourBitBUFFER to output channel 0. Carries the bitwise BUFFER of regB and regA.

mode:
    Connects control logic to FourBitAddSum module. Determines whether addition or subtraction is performed.

lastcarry:
    Connects FourBitAddSub module carry output to control logic. Indicates final carry bit for addition/subtraction operations.

outputADDSUB:
    Connects FourBitAddSub module output to channels 2 and 3. Carries the result of addition or subtraction result of regB and regA.

outputDIV:
    Connects FourBitDiv module output to channel 5. Carries integer division result of regB and regA

outputMOD:
    Connects FourBitMod module output to channel 6. Carries mod result of regB and regA.

outputMULT:
    Connects FourBitMult module output to channel 4. Carries multiplication result of regB and regA.
    
AddError:
    Connects FourBitAddSub module to to error output bit 0. Signals overflow or underflow.

ZeroError:
    Connects FourBitDiv and FourBitMod modules to error output bit 1. Signals divide by 0.

regA:
    Connects input processing logic to all AL module inputs. Holds processes value of input A.

regB:
    Connects the accumjlators lower bits to all AL module inputs. Holds current accumulator value.

next:
    Connects multiplexor output to the accumulator input. Carries the next value to be stored in the accumulator on the next clock cycle.

cur:
    Connects the accumulator output to control logic. Carries the current stored value in the accumulator register (DFF)
